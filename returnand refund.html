Our Commitment to a Fair Gemstone Return Policy in India

Welcome to Sarfaraz Gems. We understand that a clear and fair gemstone return policy in India is crucial when you decide to buy certified gemstones online. Our commitment is to provide a straightforward and trustworthy process, ensuring your investment is protected. This policy outlines the procedures for returns, exchanges, and refunds, giving you the confidence to shop for valuable items like a Pukhraj stone or an original Neelam stone without hesitation. We believe our transparent approach makes us the best online gemstone store in India.

7-Day Return & Exchange Policy

If your purchase does not meet your expectations, you are welcome to initiate a return or exchange within 7 days of the delivery date. This is a core part of our gemstone return policy, designed to give you ample time to inspect your purchase. To be eligible for a 100% money-back guarantee on the gemstone price, the item must meet the following criteria:

The item must be in its original, unused, and undamaged condition, with no signs of wear.
All original packaging, tags, and crucially, the original lab certificate must be included.
Custom-made jewelry, engraved items, or personalized orders are not eligible for return under this policy.

Key Conditions for a Successful Return

Gemstone Condition Inspection

All returned items are subject to a thorough inspection by our expert gemologists. This step is vital to our gemstone return policy to ensure the authenticity and condition of the returned product. Once we confirm the item is in its original, unaltered state, we will promptly proceed with your refund or exchange.

Mandatory Unboxing Video

To protect both you and our business from transit-related issues, a clear, unedited unboxing video is absolutely required for any claims of receiving a damaged, defective, or incorrect item. The video must show the package being opened from its sealed state. This is a mandatory step for a swift and successful resolution.

Return Shipping Responsibility

The customer is responsible for the costs and safety of return shipping. We highly recommend using a trusted courier service with tracking and insurance, such as Blue Dart or Delhivery, to protect your valuable item. Please note that original shipping fees and any COD charges are non-refundable.

Verifying Your Original Gemstone

Every gemstone we sell comes with a certificate of authenticity from a reputable lab. This certificate is your assurance of quality and a key component of our gemstone return policy. It must be returned in its original condition.

The Refund & Exchange Process in India

1

Initiate Your Return

Contact our support team via email or phone within 7 days of delivery to receive a Return Authorization Number (RAN) and detailed shipping instructions.

2

Securely Ship the Item

Carefully package the original gemstone with all its materials, including the lab certificate, and ship it back to the address we provide, clearly marking the RAN on the package.

3

Receive Your Refund

After a successful inspection, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days. We will notify you via email once the refund is initiated.

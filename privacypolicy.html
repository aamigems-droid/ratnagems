Introduction

Welcome to Sarfaraz Gems. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Sarfaraz Gems ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and safeguards your information when you visit our website or make a purchase. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

To provide our services and improve your experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiers: Your name, shipping and billing addresses, email address, and phone number.
  • Transaction Details: Information about the products you purchase. Please note, sensitive payment details are processed securely by our payment partners (e.g., Razorpay, PayPal) and are not stored on our servers.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected automatically through cookies to enhance site functionality.
  • Communications: Any correspondence you have with our customer support team.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following key purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: To process payments, arrange shipping, and deliver your orders.
  • Customer Support: To communicate with you about your orders and answer your questions.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze website usage and improve our offerings.
  • Security: To prevent fraud and protect the security of our website and customers.
  • Legal Compliance: To meet our legal and regulatory obligations.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses essential and analytical cookies to function correctly and enhance your experience. You have full control over non-essential cookies via the consent banner that appears when you first visit our site.

Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with trusted partners essential for our operations, including:

  • Payment Processors: To securely handle transactions.
  • Shipping Partners: To deliver your orders to your doorstep.
  • Analytics Providers: To help us understand website traffic and improve our services.

Data Security

We take the security of your data very seriously. Our website is secured with HTTPS encryption, and we employ robust technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights and Choices

You have rights over your personal data. You can access and update your information by logging into your account. You may also request the deletion of your account or opt-out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.

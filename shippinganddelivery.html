Our Commitment to Secure Delivery

At Sarfaraz Gems, we understand that the secure and timely delivery of your chosen items is a critical part of the gemstone online shopping experience. This policy outlines our shipping process, ensuring transparency and peace of mind from the moment you place your order to the moment it arrives at your doorstep.

Shipping Timelines Across India

We are proud to offer reliable shipping to every corner of India. Our delivery timelines are as follows:

Metro Cities

6–7 Business Days

Orders to major metropolitan areas are delivered promptly via our trusted courier partners.

Non-Metro & Remote Areas

8–9 Business Days

For other regions, we use reliable partners, including India Post, to ensure safe and secure delivery.

Our Shipping Partners

To ensure the best possible service, we work with a network of India's most reputable logistics providers, including:

  • Blue Dart
  • Delhivery
  • Amazon Shipping
  • Shiprocket
  • India Post (for remote areas)

Order Processing & Tracking

Order Processing (1-2 Days)

Before dispatch, every order undergoes a final quality check and is carefully packaged. This process typically takes 1–2 business days.

Real-Time Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS/WhatsApp, allowing you to monitor your shipment's journey in real-time.

Additional Certification

If you request additional lab reports for your gemstone, please allow up to 7 extra business days for processing and documentation.

Fully Insured & Secure Shipping

Every order shipped from Sarfaraz Gems is fully insured against loss or damage during transit, ensuring your investment is completely protected. We use secure, tamper-proof packaging to safeguard your valuable items. In the rare event of a damaged package, please refer to our Return and Refund Policy and contact us immediately.
